Who we are

A foundation built on belief in human potential

Empowering 12,400+ children since 2010
14 Years of
Service

BrightFuture Foundation was started by a small group of teachers in Mysuru who saw firsthand how poverty forces children to drop out of school. Today, we operate across Karnataka, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Bihar, Odisha, and Assam.

Our approach is holistic: we don't just provide money. We surround each child with mentors, learning materials, career counselling, and a community that believes in them.

  • Merit & need-based scholarships
    Covering school fees, books, uniforms, and digital devices for qualifying students.
  • Dedicated mentorship network
    1,200+ volunteer mentors from industry and academia guiding students weekly.
  • Transparent governance
    80G registered. Annual reports and fund-use disclosures published every quarter.
